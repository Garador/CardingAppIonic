About Extreme Cards

Extreme Cards is an app created for the purpose of allowing users to share and receive contact cards given away by individuals whom you trust, with a simple method of adding (via ID), instead of writing lengthy and often time-consuming cards.

These cards can be used to maintain your selected contacts updated with the newest information to be provided to them. Also, if you wish to stop someone from seeing the card, you can delete it.

FAQ

Q: How do I add a card someone's sharing to me?
A: Just go to "Shared Cards" and click the bottom-right button to begin adding a card. If it's a private card, you need the code the person who's sharing the card with you has. Otherwise, you won't be able to add it.

Q: How do I create a card to share to someone?
A: Go to Owned Cards, click the bottom-right button, and you should be able to create your card data. Keep in mind that if you choose the card to be private, you'll have to provide a password at the time of the creation.

Q: How do I share a card I own?
A: On your shared cards, you can swipe-left them, and you'll see a series of options. In there you can choose "Share". You can share it via ID, or you can download a .vcf (contact card) file from our servers with the contact information via clicking on "Get V-Card", and then, click on "Download" button that will appear

Q: How do I back up my cards?
A: You can download them via the share button, and store them as .vcf files to your device, or just send them over wherever you need to.

Q: What if there's a problem with the app? Will my cards be deleted?
A: No. Your cards will remain and whenever you re-install the app, you'll be able to access them once again.
